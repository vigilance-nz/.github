# Vigilance Ltd

New Zealand-based software company building regulatory-compliance and payment-protection products for finance, legal, and accounting teams across Aotearoa New Zealand and Australia.

Founded in 2014, Vigilance Ltd helps medium-to-large organisations reduce the time, cost, and risk of meeting their obligations under the AML/CFT Act and protect every dollar that leaves their bank accounts.

---

## Our Products

### [VigilantPay](https://vigilantpay.com)

An AI-powered payment-protection platform that scans every line of every accounts-payable and payroll file for fraud, error, and anomaly — before the money leaves the building.

**Capabilities**

- **Real-time anomaly detection** — proprietary AI scoring across AP and payroll payment files
- **Cross-system verification** — reconciles payment files against ERP and payroll source data
- **Automatic quarantine** — isolates suspicious transactions without holding up the rest of the run
- **Payroll fraud & ghost-employee detection**
- **Supplier / vendor change verification** — catches bank-account-change fraud at the source
- **Cross-border & FX payment protection**
- **Segregation of duties & approval workflows**
- **On-demand payment audit** with real-time risk reporting

**Built for** CFOs, financial controllers, AP teams, and boards in healthcare, logistics, education, government, not-for-profit, and retail — including organisations running multi-entity or shared-services models.

**Integrations** SAP, Oracle NetSuite, Microsoft Dynamics 365, MYOB, Xero, Pronto, Odoo, Epicor, JD Edwards, and MS Access.

**Implementation** Live within hours — no long project.

**Scale** 40+ enterprise customers; billions of dollars in payments protected each year.

### [Vigilance AML](https://vigilance.co.nz)

A comprehensive Anti-Money-Laundering and Counter-Financing of Terrorism (AML/CFT) compliance platform purpose-built for New Zealand reporting entities.

**Capabilities**

- **Customer Due Diligence (CDD)** — customisable workflows for standard, simplified, and enhanced due diligence
- **Ongoing Due Diligence** — continuous monitoring of customer risk profiles
- **Transaction Monitoring & Reporting** — automated analysis and goAML/NZ Police suspicious-activity reporting
- **Annual Reports & Audit** — generation of the artefacts your supervisor expects
- **Compliance Programme** — single source of truth for policies, procedures, and version-controlled risk assessments
- **Staff Training Register** — track who has completed what, and when

**Built for** New Zealand law firms, accounting firms, and other reporting entities operating under the AML/CFT Act 2009.

**Integrations** Actionstep, NZ Companies Office, Centrix, RealMe, APLYiD, ASB, goAML, plus advisory partnerships with Strategi and Dimension GRC.

**Security** GDPR and NZ Privacy Act 2020 aligned, with regular independent penetration testing.
